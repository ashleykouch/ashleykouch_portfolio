My Education & Experiance

Education

Bachelor of Commerce - Accounting

Macquarie University (2017 - 2021)

I started with a double degree of Commerce/Arts, majoring in Accounting and Finance and Japanese. Studying commerce wasn't necessarily a choice but rather a decision made on behalf of my parents. However, throughout the degree, I found myself unsatisfied with what I was learning.

During my 3rd year of Uni, I changed from Japanese to Information Technology as I found more of a passion in that field than I did the learning the language. I learnt this course for about a year and when I reached my penultimate, I applied as a vacationer at Deloitte in audit.

After the internship, I was offered a full-time role as an Undergrad, with the prospect of attaining a Graduate role after a year. As such, I decided to drop my second degree and focus on finalising my Accounting degree to graduate and started working full-time and studying part-time for the next year.

Experiance

Experienced Analyst

Deloitte Australia - External Audit (2020 - 2022)

Being put in different teams every few weeks meant that I had to understand how to work with different people on multiple occasions and on different jobs.

As I gained more experience, there were moments during the job where I became a senior to newcomers and those just starting. I learnt to always ask about the wellbeing of others and helped where I could. Planning out the day and the priorities needed to be done and taking responsibility for any issues that may arise.

Furthermore, being in a client-facing role meant that I needed to have good communication skills with both the client and my team. Audit meant that there is a need to ask further questions and drill in on issues that have occurred.

Although I enjoyed and understood the role I was given, my passion lied elsewhere.
